Our Story

A Home Kitchen
Built on Flavour

R&R Sweet & Savoury started as a labour of love — a home kitchen in Colombo where passion for food meets the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality. Every patty, roll, tart, and cookie is carefully prepared fresh, using quality ingredients and time-honoured recipes.

We cater to families, offices, events, and parties across Colombo. Whether you need a dozen fish rolls for a quick snack or 200 assorted short eats for a wedding reception — we've got you covered.
